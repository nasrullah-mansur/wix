About

Artis Futura

Art is taken for granted and our industry has badly adjusted to the reality of monetising our work online. A few very noteworthy service providers in the industry are underpaying artists and there seems no end in sight.

I believe however that solutions can and should be discussed, developed and implemented to make a positive change for present and future artists.

These solutions wonâ€™t be found overnight, but I hope, with your feedback and collaboration, to provide you with a flexible social monetisation network that treats you fairly and with respect.

These are my aims in short:

  • You will keep full ownership of your content and your art
  • We will never stop you from making connections on Artis Futura and monetising your art away from our platform.

In return we require the following from you:

  • You will only upload or link content that you have created yourself
  • If this content includes copyrighted materials (such as background music) you have an agreement in place with the copyright owners before you upload the content
  • You will give credit where credit is due and link all persons involved in creating your art. You will be given the opportunity to link their profiles (social media or within Artis Futura) or their websites.
  • Breaches of copyright can get you banned from the platform
  • Be respectful of each other
