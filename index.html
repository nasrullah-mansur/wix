ABOUT US

Artis Futura is a project looking for a more streamlined and fairer future for artists.

Over time we will offer you an all-in-one social media and monetisation platform. However, that grand vision will take time to develop so for now we are launching what we believe we needed first:

Remote teaching is here to stay, but it doesn't have to remain so taxing on the teachers. Artis Futura has developed a WebRTC video calling app with double the bitrate for audio, offering you Real Sound quality and dynamics. A better sound will make teaching more effective and far less frustrating

To make things even better for you we have also included an equaliser to perfect the sound of your students. Settings can be saved for each individual student for your convenience.

Even though our focus so far has been on the performing arts, many of the services we are developing will be applicable for other forms of art as well.

Soon we will be welcoming all forms of art to this platform in the hope that all of us together can create a better financial future where each artist is valued.
