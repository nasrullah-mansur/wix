Our road ahead

Artis Futura aims to develop an all-in-one platform for artists by bringing social media and a choice of monetisation options together under one roof.

Our first step is focused on education as this is, for many, the most lucrative part of our careers. I see that online education is here to stay, now many families work remotely and have left the cities. However, for artists this move is less interesting as culture will, for the time being at least, remain overwhelmingly in those big cities.

I had a video calling app developed with a focus on sound quality and dynamics. We also added an equaliser so the whole sound experience can be finetuned as you wish and will therefore be less tiring for the teachers.

Your professional profile pages will help you acquire new students which you can choose to teach either online or in person. We will never restrict you from earning outside of this platform, nor charge you a commission on any work you generate away from Artis Futura.

Our next step will be to develop more advanced profiles where you can showcase your work through picture and video galleries, articles, blogs, vlogs, etc. With those profiles we will over time offer you a choice of monetisation options for your art, such as:

  • Paid subscriptions / Patrons
  • Pay per view / pay per listen
  • Rental or sales of digital products
  • Sales of physical products such as paintings, sculptures, CDs, merchandising, etc.
  • Ticket sales for concerts, in person and through live streams
  • Advertising
  • Sponsorship
  • Commissions

Further down the road, we are looking to integrate a job/freelance work portal where anyone can post offers for paid collaborations and or paid freelance work. This will also be available to members of the public and organisations that are looking for artists.

If you have any questions and or recommendations that will help us improve our service to you, please get in touch with us or contribute to the Forums where we can collaborate and build together towards a positive change.
